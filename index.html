Who is VITALIK?

Vitalik Buterin is the co-founder of Ethereum, the platform that powers DeFi, NFTs, and the entire on-chain economy. He published the Ethereum whitepaper in 2013 at age 19 and shipped the network in 2015, turning a bold idea into the world's decentralized computer.

More than a developer, he's crypto's most influential thinker, a relentless advocate for open, permissionless systems. He doesn't hype pumps; he builds infrastructure, pushes for scaling, and backs the conviction that Ethereum is the foundation everything else runs on.
